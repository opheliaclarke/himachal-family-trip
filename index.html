❄️ One honest note on snow

By 1 July the accessible snow near Manali has mostly melted (snow season is Apr–Jun). On this homeward route — Sissu & Solang — there's no guaranteed snow to play in, only snowy peaks in the distance.

The only real snow shot would be a dawn taxi up to Marhi (~3,300 m) for leftover patches — but it needs its own morning and points away from your route home. Say the word and I'll wedge a Marhi snow-morning in; otherwise the plan below treats snow as a bonus view, not a stop.

✅ Your questions, answered

Naggar Castle · Skip 2026

Naggar Castle — closed.

Shut for a multi-year ₹11.57-cr restoration; still under work in March 2026, reopening ~2027–28. Removed from the plan — but you can still eat brilliantly in Naggar (see food).

Crowds · You're right

Manali town is chaos — so we sleep outside it.

May 2026 saw 24,000+ vehicles/week through the Atal Tunnel; post-flood jams hit 4–6 hrs. You only dip into Manali for two quick sights and sleep in a quiet orchard/dome stay (see “Where to sleep”).

Manikaran · Skip in July

Manikaran — dropped for a July trip.

I added it earlier, but the monsoon makes the Parvati Valley dangerous: flash-flood deaths near Manikaran (Mar 2025) and Kasol (Aug 2025) on a single dead-end road. Not worth the risk with a toddler in July. Save it for a dry-season trip.

  • Midday: lunch + a little Mall Road shopping.
  • Afternoon: drive through the Atal Tunnel to Sissu (~1.5 hr) — Sissu waterfall & lake.
  • Dinner & overnight in Sissu.

🚗 ~2.5 hr total, easy roads. Sissu is ~3,100 m — keep an eye on your son overnight.

Day 3 · Fri 3 Jul

Solang Valley fun, then start the drive home

🏨 Stay: Mandi (a hotel halfway home)

  • Morning: drive back through the tunnel to Solang Valley (~1 hr). Ropeway/gondola, pony ride, snow-zone activities.
  • Lunch at Solang, then begin the drive home.
  • Evening: overnight at Mandi (~3.5 hr from Solang).

🚗 Solang→Mandi ~3.5 hr. Don't push to Chandigarh today — that leg is 9–11 hr in monsoon, too long after Solang with a toddler.

Day 4 · Sat 4 Jul

Mandi → Chandigarh → home

🏨 Stay: home

  • Morning: drive Mandi → Chandigarh (~200 km, ~4.5 hr).
  • Continue ~200 km on to your hometown — easier, faster roads after Chandigarh.

🚗 A full driving day — start early, build in a monsoon buffer.

🚖 Snow add-on logistics (only if you add a Marhi morning)

Permit & taxi

  • Rohtang/Marhi needs a permit — apply online at rohtangpermits.hp.gov.in (₹550/car, car must be <10 yrs + valid PUC).
  • Out-of-state cars face a tiny 100/day “Special Permit” quota that sells out by noon — so hire a local Manali taxi (₹4,000–6,500); they sort the permit. Confirm it's included.
  • Often closed Tuesdays for maintenance — check day-of.
  • Sissu/Atal Tunnel needs no permit — self-drive freely (quick free e-Aagman online).

Gear & the toddler

  • Rent snow suit + gumboots before Solang (~₹100–300 pp) — but bring your son's own waterproof suit, boots & mittens (stall sizing for toddlers is unreliable).
  • Pack spare dry clothes, sunscreen & sunglasses (snow glare).
  • Acclimatise a day in Manali first; day-trip only, sleep low; descend if he gets unusually fussy/sleepy or off his food.
  • Keep snow play to 1–2 hours.
Monsoon driving: the Mandi–Kullu–Manali highway slips somewhere most July weeks. Build a slack day, never drive in heavy rain or at night, avoid riverside stretches when the Beas is high, and check road status every morning.
